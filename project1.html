TEMPERATURE ACQUISITION CARD

This project consists in designing a temperature acquisition card. The temperature is first converted into an electrical signal by the sensor itself. It is then formed as a voltage signal and transmitted to the microcontroller for conversion into a digital value. The value will be sent to the LCD and the graphical interface through a serial communication link.
Software language: C, C#.
IDE: mikroC, Microsoft Visual Studio.

MATERIEL
PIC16F877 microcontroller
LM35 temperature sensor
MAX232 circuit
LCD screen
RS232 connection
L7805 voltage regulator
Crystal oscillator
