FPGA IMPLEMENTATION FOR REAL TIME SOBEL EDGE DETECTOR

My project is an FPGA based real-time Sobel Edge Detector. I have used a TRDB-D5M CMOS camera that captures a video in real time. This video is processed in the FPGA and I used Verilog to implement the filter in hardware. The image continuously comes in from CMOS, and after processing, it continuously exits from VGA.
